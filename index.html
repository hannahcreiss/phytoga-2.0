About

PhyToga is a blended exercise series, combining the sound, evidence based principles of physical therapy with the ancient benefits of yoga. The program provides balance in the body and efficiency of movement through:

functional movement patterns

yoga sequences

neuromuscular reeducation

stability/strength training

balance and proprioception training

education on moving with mindfulness and decreased compensation

stretching, breathing and relaxation techniques

Format

The program is taught by a licensed Physical Therapist Assistant and RYT (registered yoga teacher), who is trained in SFMA (Selective Functional Movement Assessment) and FMS certified (Functional Movement Screen). She brings her wide range of education and experience into her customized classes and private sessions, creating a safe environment to grow strong, stable and balanced bodies.

Private sessions are customized for the individual, perfect for those who require extra attention to address dysfunctional movement patterns and personal goals, using SFMA (Selective Functional Movement Assessment) and FMS (Functional Movement Screen) as assessment tools when deemed appropriate.

Classes and private/semi private sessions are provided at the Integrated Healing Center in Paoli or in the privacy of your own home.

About the Instructor

Christine Sturgis, mother of five, sports enthusiast and yoga practitioner of 20+ years, has spent the last 10 years developing a career applying two of her greatest passions: helping others and movement.
